Jingpei Lu
M.S. in Intelligent Systems, Robotics and Control
University of California, San Diego
jil360 at ucsd.edu

Research Interests: Robotics, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning

I am a Master's student at ECE Department at UC San Diego, where I am a research assistant at Advanced Robotics and Controls Lab (ARClab) under the supervision of Prof. Michael Yip. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from UC San Diego, and doing my undergraduate research at Statistical Visual Computing Laboratory (SVCL).
Starting in 2019, I have also been closely collaborating with Educational Vision Technologies (EVT) in developing the online learning system using Machine Learning techniques.
I have a broad interest in Computer Vision and Robotics. Currently, my research focus on solving perception problems for surgical robotics using deep learning techniques.
