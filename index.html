Jingpei Lu

I am currently a Ph.D candidate at Advanced Robotics and Controls Lab (ARClab), advised by Prof. Michael Yip. I received my B.S./M.S degree in Electrical Engineering from UC San Diego.

Google Scholar  /  Email  /  Misc.

Research

I have a broad interest in Computer Vision and Robotics. Most of my research is about robotic perception for automation and control, including their application on surgical robots. My work spans 2D and 3D tracking, reconstruction, and optimization.
